Arun makes working fun & efficient

A graduate in B.Tech Information Technology from Chennai working as Application Development Senior Analyst at Accenture India. Involved in development of Micro services using Spring Boot and Go language. Implemented BDD framework Karate and Gatling for performance testing and process improvement. Worked as developer in Sterling IBM Integrator to develop interfaces for file transfers, encryption and decryption of files using secured protocols and archival of files on Google cloud Storage Expertise in working on Big query and management of Storage Buckets on Google Cloud Platform. Experienced in Terraform, Jenkins, Open Shift, Bit Bucket, gitlab, Jira , Confluence and Oracle and MongoDB. Interested in philanthropic activities, Co founder of free hands, a small initiative to help the poor through our friends and well wishers. Co founder of knowledge clown, a blogging website to get bytes of information from various fields and domains. Been part of the National Service Scheme during the college days. I have organized blood donation, tree plantation and many other camps. We have built around 10 toilets in the remote villages of Chengalpet, Tamilnadu under Swatch Bharat Abhiyan Scheme of Govt of India. Wish to become a leading Application Developer and achieve the targets of my own at regular intervals. Aim in life is to involve in a job that benefits the society and the poorer section of the people in some way.
